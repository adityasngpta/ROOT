Stink bugs are a common agricultural pest that can cause significant damage to a variety of crops, including vegetables, fruits, and field crops. They feed on the plant tissues, piercing the leaves, stems, or fruits and extracting the sap. This can lead to distorted growth, discoloration, and even premature dropping of the affected plant parts.

While the leaf appears healthy at the moment, continued stink bug infestation can lead to reduced plant vigor, decreased yields, and increased susceptibility to other pests and diseases.
