1. Monitoring: Regularly inspect the crop for the presence of grasshoppers, egg masses, and any signs of feeding damage. This will help identify the extent of the infestation and guide the appropriate control measures.

2. Mechanical control: Consider using physical barriers, such as row covers or netting, to prevent grasshoppers from accessing the crop. This can be an effective preventive measure, especially for smaller-scale operations.
